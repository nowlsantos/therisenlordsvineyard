Mission

To bear witness to the Resurrection of Jesus Christ. And to bear witness also to the Immaculate Conception of the Blessed Virgin Mary.

 Vision

The Risen Lord's Vineyard is a Community of witnesses of the Risen Lord and devoted children of the Blessed Virgin Mary making a difference in the lives of people by sharing the love and Word of God, thus strengthening the church built by Christ.

Theme

Rise up to a renewed life in Christ and be transformed by His love.
